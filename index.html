Hello!
We're Dawie and Lyza, Your
Compassionate Counselors!

Our journey into counseling began from a place of personal pain and growth. Both of us have faced deep trauma and depression, and it was through these dark moments that we felt called to equip ourselves with the knowledge and tools to not only heal but to help others who are broken and struggling. We committed to studying and training, determined to turn our experiences into a mission of hope and restoration. For the past two years, we’ve been counseling together as a couple on a professional level, and in that time, we’ve seen God work in miraculous ways. The breakthroughs and healing that have taken place in the lives of those we counsel are not by our strength, but by His grace. We give all the glory to God for the transformation we’ve witnessed, and we are honored to continue helping others find their path to healing and peace.

A Helping Hand
Can Save a Life!

Mental Health First Aid training equips youth and adults with the knowledge and skills to recognize early signs of mental health issues and crises, such as anxiety, depression, or suicidal thoughts. The training teaches practical strategies for providing immediate support, offering reassurance, and guiding the person toward professional help. Participants learn how to listen non-judgmentally, encourage self-help strategies, and respond effectively in emergencies. It's designed for everyone, fostering a compassionate community capable of supporting mental well-being and reducing stigma surrounding mental health challenges.
